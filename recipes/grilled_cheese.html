Grilled Cheese

Description

The grilled cheese is a hot cheesy sandwich served with tomato soup.


Ingredients

  • Bread (preferebly Sourdough
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Butter
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on the outside of the bread.
  2. Add grated Cheddar and Sliced Mozzarella
  3. Butter both open sides of bread slices
  4. Place the sandwich on cast iron skillet on low flame
  5. Cover the lod of pan. AFter 3-5min flip and close the lid again
  6. Serve hot with tomato soup
