Croissants

Description

A croissant is a French pastry made from puff pastry in a crescent shape.


Ingredients

  • 500g All purpose Flour
  • 140g Water
  • 140g Whole Milk
  • 55g Sugar
  • 40g unsalted Butter
  • 11g instant yeast
  • 12g salt
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Make easy dough from butter, flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and milk..
  2. Roll out dough into a large rectangle
  3. Make the butter layer. Enclose the butter layer inside the dough.
  4. Roll out the dough into another large rectangle, then fold it back together. Repeat this 3 times.
  5. Cut the rectangle into long Triangles and roll them into croissants. Egg wash the croissants.
  6. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes
